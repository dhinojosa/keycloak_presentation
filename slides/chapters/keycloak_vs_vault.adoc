== Keycloak vs Vault
image::iStock-1181695869.jpg[background, size=cover]

=== Keycloak vs Vault

[cols="35b,65"]
|===
| Tool | Job

| Keycloak
| Login, users, sessions, roles, tokens, federation

| Vault
| Secrets, encryption keys, leases, rotation, dynamic credentials
|===

=== Badge Office vs Safe

* Keycloak is the badge office
* Vault is the safe
* A badge proves who you are and what doors you can open
* A safe protects valuables and releases them under policy

=== The Rule

* Keycloak proves identity
* Keycloak issues Access Tokens
* Vault protects secrets
* Vault issues secret material
* They solve different security problems

== Self-Hosted Identity

=== Why Self-Hosted Identity?

* One login for your own services
* Fewer password databases
* Central roles and groups
* Standard OIDC and SAML integration
* Your trust boundary stays yours

* CI/CD
* Websites
