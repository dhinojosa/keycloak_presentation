=== Why Identity is Hard

* "Who are you?" vs. "What are you allowed to do?"
* Authentication vs. Authorization vs Identity
* Why rolling your own auth is a bad idea
* Common failure modes:
** Shared secrets everywhere
** Token sprawl
** Auth logic leaking into domain code
** Identity in monoliths vs distributed systems
